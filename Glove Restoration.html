Why restore gloves?

Glove restoration is important for several reasons. Firstly, it significantly extends the lifespan of gloves, allowing them to be used for many more years, which is both cost-effective and environmentally friendly. Additionally, restoration helps maintain the functionality of gloves, ensuring they retain their flexibility, comfort, and grip for various activities, whether for work, sports, or fashion.

our services

Glove services encompass cleaning, restoration, repair, and customization to maintain and enhance the longevity and appearance of gloves. Cleaning services focus on removing dirt, stains, and oils, using specialized products for leather or delicate fabrics to keep the gloves in optimal condition.

Polishing

To polish an old leather glove, start by cleaning it with a soft, dry cloth to remove any dirt or dust. For deeper cleaning, use a damp cloth or a specialized leather cleaner, but avoid soaking the glove. Once clean, apply a small amount of leather conditioner or glove oil and let it absorb for 10-15 minutes to moisturize the leather.

Restoration

Restoring old leather gloves involves cleaning, conditioning, and reviving their flexibility and appearance. Start by gently cleaning the gloves with a soft, damp cloth or a leather cleaner to remove any dirt or grime. Be careful not to oversaturate the leather.

Repairing

Repairing old gloves requires attention to detail to restore their functionality and appearance. Start by inspecting the gloves for any damage, such as tears, loose stitching, or worn-out areas. For leather gloves, small holes or tears can be repaired using leather patches or leather adhesive.
